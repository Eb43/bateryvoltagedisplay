Battery Voltage Display is a lightweight Android app that shows real-time battery voltage in the system status bar and notification area.

It uses shows accurate voltage readings in persistent icon visible over other apps, helping users monitor battery health and performance.

  This app is:

    - Ad-free
    - Efficient and low-resource
    - Allows you to activate autorun
    - Compatible with Android 8 and above

  Ideal for users who want a simple, unobtrusive way to keep an eye on battery voltage without unnecessary permissions or background services.
